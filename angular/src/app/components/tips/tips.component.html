Tips
Water :
  • Clean containers
  • Original containers (purchase commercially)
  • Ten drops CL per gallon, stand for 30-60 minutes, boil for 1 min
  • Reuse water for sanitation purposes
  • Treat rain water and ground water
  • Cool before drinking
  • Store water prepared filtering only for 6 months
  • Distilling to make saltwater become drinkable

Food :
  • Keep fresh
  • Kept in sealed air tight containers
  • Easily prepared
  • Expiration date

Clothing & Bedding :
  • Always include warm clothing
  • Sleeping bags & mats suited to the weather
  • Use padded ground pads for both comfort and warmth
  • Jackets for 3 days for each person

First Aid and Medications :
  • Special needs households should consider additional items
  • Latex gloves
  • Sterile dressings with tape and bandages
  • Cleansing agents
  • Antibiotic ointment
  • Eye wash
  • Thermometer
  • Splinting supplies
  • Scissors and tweezers
  • Aspirin or non-aspirin pain relievers
  • Antacids
  • Anti-diarrheals
  • Benadryl

Miscellaneous Items :
  • Flash lights with extra batteries
  • Tools, a wrench or pliers, along with a hammer and screwdrivers
  • Camp knife
  • Matches in a waterproof container
  • Small mirror for signaling or other uses
  • Whistle to signal for help
  • Moist towelettes
  • Garbage bags and buckets (for sanitation, toileting needs, and other uses)
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Household bleach
  • Copies of important personal and family papers
  • Cash
  • Paper & pencil
  • Favorite books, games, or puzzles for all member of the household

Storage :
  • Cool, dry, protected place
  • Easy access in an emergency

Jump Bag :
  • Prepare an evacuation duffle bag or backpack
